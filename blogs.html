Poetry Corner

I don't remember the day and place I met you but "She is just another girl in the world full of unique souls " I thought to myself, but every now and then when I see that the golden rays of sunset rest upon your face, a flame ignites, affirming in my heart, 'She is yours'. I don't know if there's one more guy in your mind,
I don't know if our love's the one you'd find,
But in this uncertainty, I'll still hold tight,
Hoping our connection shines in the darkest night.
Amidst our playful exchanges, blushes bloom like roses in the garden of our games.
My friends enquire "Who is her?" and I reply "A perfect blend of American pop and some desi traditional Bollywood hits".
"Mmmm Someone who is so important and perhaps too sweet for me." I whisper.
Amidst the scorching heat, you appeared like a cool breeze, gently carrying me away from the pain.
Do the things that you like and listen to your silly playlists. Finish your nonsensical assignments and when your head is empty at 3 in the morning let me linger in your thoughts before closing your chocolate brown eyes as you surrender to sleep.
Could you be my final muse as I am yours, lingering in the twilight of thoughts?
